ABOUT

Endure By Faith (EBF) was founded in 2022 by a leadership team deeply engaged in the military and veteran community. Through our own transitions and collaborations, we discovered a vast number of valuable resources designed to support service members—but navigating them was often overwhelming. We saw an opportunity to bring clarity and accessibility to these resources, ensuring that all military personnel—whether Active Duty, Transitioning, or Veteran—have the tools they need to succeed.


At EBF, we are committed to simplifying the process by consolidating vetted and trusted resources in one place, making it easier for military members to access career, education, and support services that truly make a difference. Beyond resource integration, EBF actively engages in government contracting, recruiting, and staffing initiatives, fostering meaningful opportunities for veterans and military personnel in the workforce.


Our mission is to empower those who have served by providing a clear pathway to success through collaboration, innovation, and unwavering support.
